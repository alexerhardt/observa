### Notes

- Eurostat ESSPROS covers old-age and survivors spending plus beneficiaries, with the clearest official cross-country structure.
- OECD indicator pages are useful for comparative pension spending and net replacement rates and point into the Data Explorer.
- Seguridad Social statistics pages provide Spain-specific pension payroll, beneficiaries, and flow tables with straightforward official downloads.
- AIReF remains analytically relevant for interpretation, but I excluded it from Links because it is not a clear open-data/API source.
