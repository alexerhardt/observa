### Notes

- INE is the strongest Spain-specific source and already exposes migration balance tables plus methodology.
- Eurostat gives the cleanest comparable EU coverage and explicitly documents net migration plus statistical adjustment.
- OECD migration datasets look suitable for cross-country comparison, but the most directly useful pages are the dataset entry points rather than the general migration landing page.
- World Bank net migration is a fallback comparative series; it is easy to access but less aligned with Spain-specific administrative detail.
