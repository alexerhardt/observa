### Notes

- The INE house price index is the cleanest Spain-specific purchase-price source and is fully official.
- Eurostat gives a strong EU comparison layer, including house price index, affordability, and visual dashboards.
- OECD is useful for comparative housing prices and regional house price tracking, plus a broader affordability database.
- The rental index from INE is a viable fallback if the chart ends up focusing on rents rather than transaction prices.
