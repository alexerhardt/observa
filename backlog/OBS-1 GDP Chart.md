### Notes

- INE is the strongest Spain-specific source for annual and quarterly GDP, with direct national accounts pages and an OpenAPI entry point on the site.
- Eurostat is the best cross-country source for EU-focused GDP comparisons and has both database and visualisation entry points.
- OECD is a good comparable source for GDP growth and national accounts across OECD countries, with clear indicator and dataset pages.
- World Bank is a clean fallback for broad international comparisons, but it is less tailored than INE/Eurostat for Spain-focused charting.
