Independent project aggregating electoral surveys and data since 1977. Covers CIS barometer data, general elections, regional elections, and polling trends.

Good example of a private, niche aggregation project on Spanish data — clean dashboards, focused scope. Worth studying as a model for how a small team can build a useful data product around a specific vertical.
