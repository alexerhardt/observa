Independent organization (since 2015) analyzing sustainability indicators for Spain: climate change, biodiversity, air quality, inequality, housing, circular economy.

Publishes annual sector and thematic reports. Interesting as a reference for how an independent org can build authority around a thematic vertical with data + reports.
