Foundation dedicated to fact-checking and combating disinformation. Team of 50+ professionals.

Key data-relevant sections:
- **Maldito Dato**: dedicated data verification unit
- **Maldita Hemeroteca**: tracks political contradictions over time
- **Maldita Ciencia**: scientific claim verification

Partner in Facebook's external verification program. Interesting as a reference for how verified data can be presented to a broad audience — they've achieved significant reach in Spain.

More journalistic than analytical, but their approach to data verification and presentation is worth studying.
