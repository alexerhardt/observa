Electoral data portal with open data on results, polls, and political analysis. Interactive visualizations for Spanish electoral data.

Part of a cluster of Spanish electoral data sites (along with Electográfica, Electomania, Porcentual, Colmena de Datos) that shows there's a real audience for niche data aggregation in Spain.
